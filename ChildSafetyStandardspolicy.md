# Child Safety Standards Policy (CSAE)

**Last updated:** February 03, 2026

## Overview

This repository documents the **Child Safety Standards Policy** for **Hangukverse**, operated by **Lumantara LTD**.  
This policy exists separately from the general Privacy Policy and is designed to comply with **Google Play Child Safety Standards**, including requirements related to **Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)**.

Our platform maintains **zero tolerance** toward any form of child exploitation or abuse.

---

## Commitment to Child Safety

We are committed to creating and maintaining a safe environment for all users.  
Any content or behavior that endangers minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate enforcement actions.

Child safety is embedded into our:
- Platform design
- Content moderation practices
- Reporting workflows
- Legal compliance procedures

---

## Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly forbidden on Hangukverse:

- Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form
- Sexualized content involving minors (real, altered, animated, or AI-generated)
- Grooming or exploitative behavior toward minors
- Solicitation, coercion, or manipulation of children
- Requests for sexual images, videos, or conversations involving minors

---

## Prevention & Moderation

We use a combination of automated and manual safeguards, including:

- Automated detection systems for high-risk content
- Human moderation and review processes
- User reporting mechanisms
- Account monitoring and behavioral analysis

Content may be reviewed **before or after publication**.

---

## Reporting Mechanisms

### User Reporting
Users can report suspected child exploitation or unsafe behavior directly through:
- In-app reporting tools
- Official support and contact channels

### Mandatory Reporting
When required by law, we:
- Preserve relevant data
- Report confirmed or suspected CSAE to appropriate authorities
- Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

---

## Enforcement Actions

Violations of this policy may result in:

- Immediate removal of content
- Temporary or permanent account suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Reporting to law enforcement or child protection authorities

Repeat or severe violations result in permanent bans.

---

## Age Protection

- Hangukverse is **not intended for users under 16 years of age**
- We do not knowingly allow minors to create accounts
- Accounts identified as belonging to minors may be suspended or deleted
- Any improperly collected data related to minors is removed promptly

---

## Data Handling for Safety Investigations

Data related to child safety enforcement is:
- Access-restricted
- Used only for moderation, safety, and legal compliance
- Retained only as long as legally or operationally required

We do **not** use such data for advertising or profiling.

---

## Continuous Improvement

We regularly update our safety practices by:
- Reviewing moderation guidelines
- Training staff on CSAE prevention
- Updating systems to address emerging threats

---

## Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect:
- Legal or regulatory changes
- Platform feature updates
- Improvements to safety standards

Updates become effective once published.
